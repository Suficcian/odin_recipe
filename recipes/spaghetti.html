Easy Spaghetti Recipe

Description

An easy spaghetti recipe that saves me lots of time. This dish satisfies everyone in the family. Try it tonight!

Ingredients

  • 0.5 lb spaghetti noodles
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti noodles according to the package directions.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the marinara sauce and add the fresh basil.
  3. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley.
  4. Season with red pepper flakes if desired.
  5. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti noodles.
