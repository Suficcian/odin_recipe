Easy Cookies Recipe

Description

An easy cookies recipe that saves me lots of time. This dish satisfies everyone in the family. Try it tonight!

Making perfect homemade cookies doesn’t have to be tedious. This top-rated easy cookies recipe comes together quickly with a relatively short ingredient list.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
