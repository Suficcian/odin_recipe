Easy Lasagna Recipe

Description

An easy lasagna recipe that saves me lots of time. This dish satisfies everyone in the family. Try it tonight!

Making perfect homemade lasagna doesn’t have to be tedious. This top-rated easy lasagna recipe comes together quickly with a relatively short ingredient list.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil

Instructions

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package directions.
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain the excess fat.
  3. Add the marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese and egg.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  7. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
